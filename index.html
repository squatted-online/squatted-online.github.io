squatted.online

online and offline exhibition space for
the wrong digital art biennale

squatted.online embassy reflects on the possibilities of squatting a digital space ♦ in the context of our exhibition, we try to look at the internet as an architectural space where each webpage is a building ♦ in this perspective, we are interested in the capabilities of squats to organize themselves and produce counterculture while occupying a building ♦ are such means possible in a digital environment? ♦ we invited a group of artists to reflect on such topics; each one of them showing different ways to use, misuse or occupy webpages, inviting on a critical reflection of the contemporary use of the internet ♦
