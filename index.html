squatted.online

online and offline exhibition space for
the wrong digital art biennale

squatted.online embassy reflects on the possibilities of squatting digital spaces ♦ in the context of our exhibition, we try to look at the internet as an architectural space where each webpage is a building ♦ in this perspective, we are interested in the capabilities of digital squats to organize themselves and produce counterculture, while occupying buildings ♦ are such proposals possible in a digital space? ♦ we invited a group of artists to reflect on such topics; each one of them showing ways to use, misuse or occupy webpages that invite on critical reflection of the contemporary use of the internet.
