squatted.online


online and offline exhibition space for
the wrong digital art biennale

squatted.online embassy reflects on the possibilities of squatting digital spaces.
In the context of our exhibition we try to look at the internet as an architectural space where each webpage is a building.
In this perspective, we are interested in the capabilities of digital squats to organize themselves and produce counterculture, while occupying buildings.
Are such proposal possible in a digital space?
We invited a group of artists to reflect on such topics; each one of them showing ways to use, misuse or occupy webpages that invite on crititcal reflection of the contemporary use of the internet.
