Before installing a video surveillance system, you have to decide why an object or space need to be watched over and if it is worth the investment. Moreover, because cameras are an indirect tool to control a space, it should only be used if no other options are available. This contradiction: the desire to secure something and the waive of direct control on it, is the starting point of this work.
